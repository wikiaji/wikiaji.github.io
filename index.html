About Us

OUR COMPANY

Initially our company was engaged in manufacturing where we processed derivative products of corn, cloves, copra, candlenut and Coconut Charcoal Briquettes. after that our company developed and developed into a supplier in Indonesia, to process more derivative products.

Celebes Indo Trade is one of the leading suppliers of agricultural products in Indonesia. We have been established for more than 5 years in supplying products in the world. We supply corn, copra, candlenut, cloves and coconut charcoal briquettes.

Celebes Indo Trade is a privately owned company with a very diverse customer base. Our dedicated production and quality control teams have worked tirelessly to deliver the highest quality products that our customers expect. Supported by more than 50 employees and more than 200 farmers, as well as the best technology for our industry. Ultimately, it is our top priority to meet our customers' satisfaction and expectations.

OUR STRATEGY FOR SUCCESS

We have a strategy for success, namely by developing a network of farmers across the plantations that we manage. And we ensure readiness in our processing plants, to be able to produce the best products with international quality.

Currently our company has partnered with more than 200 farmers in eastern Indonesia, and the plantation land that we manage is more than 10,500 hectares, and agricultural supplies. We also help the local economy to develop and make welfare for existing farmers.
